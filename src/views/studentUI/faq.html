Getting Started

Signing up is easy! Just click on the "Get Started" button on our homepage and complete the registration form. You'll need to:

  1. Provide basic information (name, email, school)
  2. Complete our learning style assessment quiz
  3. Set up your profile

The whole process takes about 10-15 minutes.

Our learning style assessment is a short quiz that helps us understand how you learn best. It asks questions about:

This information helps us match you with tutors who can teach in ways that work best for you.

peerAid is completely free for all students!

We believe in accessible education for everyone, which is why we offer:

  • Unlimited peer tutoring sessions
  • Access to tutors both within your school and from partner institutions
  • All platform features including our learning style assessment and matching algorithm

Tutoring Sessions

peerAid makes it easy to find the right tutor:

  1. Our algorithm automatically suggests tutors based on your learning style and academic needs
  2. You can browse tutors by subject, availability, and ratings
  3. Each tutor profile shows their specialties, teaching style, and schedule

Tutoring sessions are through online meeting or in person and typically include:

  • Introduction and goal-setting for the session
  • Interactive lesson tailored to your learning style
  • Practice problems or discussions
  • Q&A time
  • Summary of key takeaways and next steps

Sessions can be 30, 45, or 60 minutes long depending on your preference.
