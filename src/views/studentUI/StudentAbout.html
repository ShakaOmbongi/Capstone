About peerAid

Transforming the way students learn together through personalized peer tutoring.

Our Story

January: The Spark of Inspiration

As computer science seniors facing our own academic challenges, we recognized the limitations of traditional tutoring systems. peerAid was born from late-night coding sessions and a shared vision for more personalized learning.

February: First Prototype Development

We designed and built the initial peerAid prototype, creating wireframes and developing core functionality while balancing our final semester coursework. The first version featured our revolutionary learning style assessment.

March: Algorithm Breakthrough

After rigorous testing and iterations, we perfected our matching algorithm that pairs students based on complementary learning styles - becoming the heart of the peerAid experience.

April: Product Launch

We proudly unveiled the completed peerAid platform at our university's Computer Science Capstone Fair.

Our Mission

At peerAid, we believe that learning is not one-size-fits-all. Every student has a unique way of understanding and retaining information, and our mission is to connect them with the right tutor to unlock their full potential.

Our platform allows students to tutor their peers within their school or receive guidance from experienced tutors outside their institution. Through our personalized matching system, based on a learning style quiz taken during registration, we ensure that each student is paired with a tutor who best fits their needs and learning preferences.

Whether you're looking to reinforce classroom knowledge, master a challenging subject, or share your expertise to help others succeed, peerAid provides a supportive, engaging, and effective learning environment tailored just for you.

How peerAid Works

1. Take the Quiz

Complete our learning style assessment during registration to identify how you learn best.

2. Get Matched

Our algorithm pairs you with compatible tutors based on learning styles, subjects, and availability.

3. Start Learning

Schedule sessions, connect with study groups, and achieve your academic goals with personalized support.
