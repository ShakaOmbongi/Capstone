About Us

At peerAid, we believe that learning is not one-size-fits-all. Every student has a unique way of understanding and retaining information, and our mission is to connect them with the right tutor to unlock their full potential.

Our platform allows students to tutor their peers or receive guidance from experienced tutors outside their institution. Through our personalized matching system, based on a learning style quiz taken during registration, we ensure that each student is paired with a tutor who best fits their needs and learning preferences.

Whether you're looking to reinforce classroom knowledge, master a challenging subject, or share your expertise to help others succeed, peerAid provides a supportive, engaging, and effective learning environment tailored just for you.
